Spatio

Spatio-temporal data collection refers to the process of gathering and recording data that includes both spatial (geographical) and temporal (time-related) information. This type of data collection is particularly valuable for understanding how data changes and evolves over space and time, allowing for the analysis of trends, patterns, and relationships that might not be apparent in static data.

DataForms

Dataform is a service for data analysts to develop, test, version control, and schedule complex SQL workflows for data transformation in BigQuery. Dataform lets you manage data transformation in the Extraction, Loading, and Transformation (ELT) process for data integration. After raw data is extracted from source systems and loaded into BigQuery, Dataform helps you to transform it into a well-defined, tested, and documented suite of data tables.
